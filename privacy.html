Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 13, 2025

1. Overview

At NEXA Creative Labs, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when you visit our website or use our services.

  • Personal identifiers (e.g., name, email address) when you make a purchase or contact us.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver purchased products and services.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To improve user experience and website performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data only when we have a legal basis to do so, including:

  • Your consent
  • Performance of a contract
  • Legal obligations
  • Legitimate interests

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it only with trusted third parties (like payment processors and analytics providers) to help us run our business.

6. Your Rights

If you are located in the EU, UK, or California, you have specific rights regarding your personal data, including the right to:

  • Access or request a copy of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • File a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

8. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance functionality and collect usage data. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

9. Children's Privacy

NEXA does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us for immediate removal.

10. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating this page and the "Effective Date" above.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.

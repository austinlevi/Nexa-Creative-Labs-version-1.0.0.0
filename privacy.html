Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 13, 2025

1. Overview

At NEXA Creative Labs, your privacy is a priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, use our products, or engage with our services. By using NEXA, you agree to the practices outlined below.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal identifiers (e.g., name, email address, phone number)
  • Payment information (processed securely via Gumroad; we do not store this data directly)
  • Usage data (e.g., IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on site)
  • Device information (e.g., operating system, screen size, device type)
  • Communication history (when you contact us via email, chat, or forms)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us:

  • Process transactions and deliver digital products
  • Provide customer support and service updates
  • Analyze and improve platform performance
  • Personalize user experiences and content
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, or technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share information with trusted third-party services that support our operations, such as:

  • Gumroad (payment processing)
  • Google Analytics (site analytics and performance tracking)
  • Email providers and cloud infrastructure services

These providers are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We only process your data when we have a valid legal basis, including:

  • Your explicit consent
  • Contractual necessity (e.g., to deliver purchased products)
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Our legitimate business interests (e.g., improving services)

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict data processing
  • Withdraw your consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)
  • Request a copy of your data in portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and for legitimate legal or business purposes, such as resolving disputes or enforcing our terms. When no longer needed, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, so we encourage you to take personal precautions when sharing data online.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, law, or our services. When we do, we’ll update the “Effective Date” above. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or this policy, please get in touch with us.
